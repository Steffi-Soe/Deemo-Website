Deemo aspires to introduce new, fun yet compact ways to cover all Basic Statistics materials through fully animated video tutorials.

SYNOPSIS

The film begins with a quick description of the topic and an introduction to all of the names and student IDs engaged in the production before diving right into the materials' contents. This topic consists of Defining Variables which can be separated into two classes; Qualitative Variables and Quantitative Variables, then the methods used in Collecting Data, Types of Sampling Methods, and last but not least are the Types of Survey Errors.
